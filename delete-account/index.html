Delete Your Account

Last updated: April 24, 2026

You can delete your LCR account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to do it and what happens to your data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the LCR App on your device.
  2. Tap the Profile tab (bottom navigation).
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon at the bottom).
  4. Scroll down to the Konti (Account) section.
  5. Tap Siba Konti Yanjye (Delete My Account).
  6. Confirm by tapping Yego, Siba (Yes, Delete).
Note: If you see a message asking you to sign out and sign back in first, please do so and then repeat the steps above. This is a security requirement by Firebase to confirm your identity before account deletion.

What Data Is Deleted

Data Type Action Retention Period
Email address Deleted immediately None
Display name Deleted immediately None
Profile photo URL Deleted immediately None
Prayer requests & community posts Deleted immediately None
Firebase Authentication account Deleted immediately None
Saved Bible verses & notes (local) Stored locally on your device only Until you uninstall the app
App usage analytics (Firebase) Anonymised by Google/Firebase Up to 14 months (Firebase default)
